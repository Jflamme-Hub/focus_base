Privacy Policy for Focus Base

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Focus Base ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web and mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Storage

Focus Base is designed as an offline-first, client-side application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, tasks, notes, or routines to external servers.

All data you enter into the application is stored entirely locally on your personal device using web storage technologies (like localStorage). You have complete control over this data and can delete it at any time directly through the app's settings.

3. Permissions

Focus Base may request permission to send you local notifications (to remind you of tasks and routines). This process happens entirely on your device, and no notification data is tracked by or sent to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Because the app runs locally on your device, we do not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks that collect your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your email address, any updates will simply be posted to this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the app's GitHub repository or store listing.
