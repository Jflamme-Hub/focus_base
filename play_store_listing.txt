2. Long Description (Max 4000 characters)
---------------------------------------------------------
Focus Base is an ADHD-friendly, offline planner designed to support executive function, reduce time blindness, and make daily routines easier through clear visuals and distraction-free structure.

Whether you're managing schoolwork, home responsibilities, or long-term goals, Focus Base organizes everything into clean, color-coded categories so you can focus on one task at a time. The entire app runs offline, keeping your data fully private and stored only on your device.

**🌈 Accessible, Neurodivergent-Friendly Design**
⭐ Key Features
• 🎯 Dynamic Visual Urgency: Tasks intensify in color as deadlines approach, shifting from subtle cues to bold, high-contrast alerts 48 hours out—ideal for ADHD time blindness and visual learners.
• 🔁 Routine Builder: Create daily or weekly routines with nested checklists that auto-reset at midnight to support consistent habit formation.
• ⏰ Immersive Reminders: Pop-up reminders stay visible for 25 seconds and include optional sensory-friendly audio chimes to help you stay on track without being startled.
• 🏆 Gamified Motivation: Earn points for completing tasks and unlock progression badges (Bronze, Silver, Gold) to reinforce positive routines.
• 🧘 Focus Mode: A distraction-free overlay hides everything except your current task, helping reduce context switching and supporting sustained attention.
• 🎨 Customizable Categories: Assign unique colors to School, Chores, Work, Goals, and more. The interface adapts instantly to your preferences.
• 🗑️ Smart Archiving: Completed tasks clean themselves up after 7 days, and overdue items trigger a simple “Keep or Delete” prompt to prevent clutter and reduce decision fatigue.

Focus Base helps you stay organized, build habits that stick, and maintain clarity throughout your day—designed with accessibility and neurodivergent needs in mind.
